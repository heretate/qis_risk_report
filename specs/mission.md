# Mission

QIS Risk Report is a tool to generate analytics and insights on a pre-defined QIS strategy.

The QIS strategy is a FX strategy that we want to embed in our broader portfolio and as part of this process we wish to maintain daily monitoring of performance and risk of the strategy and its subcomponents which are 4 different QIS.

## Target Audience

- Portfolio managers for the purpose of insights and monitoring

## Goals

- Understand and monitor the risk-return characteristics of the strategy and its subcomponents
- Quantify the risk contribution of the strategy towards the broader portfolio
- Illustrate performance across different scenarios including both historical and synthetic situations
- Understand strategy attribution across various factors
